What Makes Us Us!

Four decades of putting people, craft, and community first. At Morningvine, we believe a great cup of coffee is about more than just the beans — it's about the hands that roast them, the conversations shared over them, and the neighborhoods they bring together.

We source our beans directly from small family farms, roast them in-house every single morning, and serve them with the kind of care you'd expect from a neighbor, not a corporation. That's the Morningvine way — always has been, always will be.

Family Owned

Three generations of the Viera family have poured their heart into every cup since 1986. No franchises, no shortcuts — just family. When Marcus Viera first fired up a hand-crank roaster in his garage, he made a promise: every cup would be roasted with intention and served with warmth.

His daughter Lena expanded that vision into a brick-and-mortar home on Maplewood Lane, and now granddaughter Ava carries the torch — bringing new ideas while honoring the traditions that made us who we are. When you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer. You're family.

Community Centered

We sponsor local artists, host weekly open-mics, and donate 5% of profits to neighborhood programs. Your coffee builds community. Every Friday night, our back room transforms into a stage for local musicians, poets, and storytellers. Every wall in the shop features rotating work from Portland artists.

We partner with Maplewood Elementary's reading program, provide free meeting space for neighborhood groups, and run a "pay-it-forward" board where regulars can buy a coffee for someone who needs one. Because a coffee shop isn't just a business — it's the living room of the neighborhood.

Our Story

From a tiny roaster in a garage to a beloved neighborhood staple.

1986

The First Roast

Marcus Viera began roasting beans in his garage with a hand-crank drum roaster and selling at the farmer's market.

2001

A Place to Gather

Opened our first brick-and-mortar shop on Maplewood Lane. The morning light through the windows inspired our name.

2014

Recognition & Growth

Named "Best Independent Roaster" by Pacific Northwest Coffee Guild. Launched direct-trade partnerships.

2026

Still Here, Still Us

Three generations later, Ava Viera leads the team. Same beans, same soul, same warm mornings.
